About Me

I enjoyed a 33 year career as a kindergarten teacher. My passion to learn, grow and help others kept me very driven during a very long career. Now I find myself retired from teaching, at age 60 and learning to be a full stack web developer @ the University of Arizona Coding Bootcamp. This is a challenge that I look forward with eager anticipation

When I am not coding you can often find me DJing a musical event, collecting music or working on my extensive video @ music collections. I love spending time with my family and the normal challenges that growing brings.
